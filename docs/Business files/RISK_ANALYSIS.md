## Business Risks

### Risk 1: Low Customer Acquisition

**Description:**
- Unable to acquire customers at target rate
- Marketing channels not effective
- Low conversion rates
- High CAC

**Probability:** Medium (40%)
**Impact:** High
**Risk Level:** High

**Mitigation Strategies:**
1. **Diversify Channels**
   - Multiple marketing channels
   - Don't rely on single channel
   - Test and optimize

2. **Improve Conversion**
   - Better messaging
   - Clearer value proposition
   - Easier onboarding
   - A/B test everything

3. **Reduce CAC**
   - Focus on organic channels
   - Referral program
   - Partnerships
   - Content marketing

4. **Adjust Strategy**
   - Pivot if needed
   - Change pricing
   - Different positioning
   - New features

**Monitoring:**
- Track CAC weekly
- Monitor conversion rates
- Review channel performance
- Adjust if below targets
